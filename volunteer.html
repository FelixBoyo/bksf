Who Can Volunteer?

Student Volunteers

Eligibility: College students above 18 years and university students pursuing a bachelor’s degree.

Experience: Gain practical experience while studying.

Graduate Volunteers

Eligibility: Individuals with a university degree and those pursuing a master’s or Ph.D.

Experience: Apply advanced knowledge and research skills.

Young Professionals

Eligibility: Those with bachelor’s, master’s, or Ph.D. degrees, currently building a career.

Experience: Enhance your professional skills in a new cultural context.

Volunteering Opportunities

Programs

Roles Include: Accounts and finance, human resource management, IT, photography, and videography.

Contribution: Support our operational and promotional activities.

Monitoring and Evaluation

Roles Include: Establishing monitoring and evaluation tools and guidelines.

Contribution: Ensure the success of our projects and measure their impact.
