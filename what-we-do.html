What we do

Boreholes for Water Supply

We are committed to drilling and maintaining boreholes to ensure a reliable supply of clean water for the Turkana community. Our efforts reduce the burden on families and enhance daily life.

Food Provision


Our food programs aim to alleviate hunger by providing nutritious meals to those in need. This includes school feeding programs and community distribution efforts to ensure no one goes hungry.

Shelter for Displaced Families

We provide temporary and permanent housing solutions for families displaced by adverse weather conditions. Our shelters offer safety, dignity, and stability as families rebuild their lives.

Mother and Child Care Support


Our support programs focus on the health and well-being of mothers and children. We offer prenatal and postnatal care, nutrition programs, and health education to build a stronger, healthier community.

Sensitization on Current Trends

We conduct programs to raise awareness about current trends affecting the community, including health and environmental challenges. Our aim is to empower individuals with the knowledge and tools they need.
